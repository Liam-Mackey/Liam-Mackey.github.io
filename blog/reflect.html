Website Reflection

1/13/16

I had a lot of fun making my own website. I experienced many challenges and defeats. One of the main challenges I had was achieving a visually appealing look using css and html alone. This upset me for a long time. Then in week 9 I had trouble bootstrapping my site. After finally accomplishing it I feel very proud of myself. I feel like I finally understand HTML and CSS as well as JavaScript!

I had a ton of fun making this website and even though It took a lot of hard work, I think I have finally made a beautiful site. I am feeling ready for DBC onsite!
